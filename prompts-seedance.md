## VIDEO A4: "Mientras dormís" (Prioridad ALTA)

**Concepto:** Contraste entre el caos de mensajes a deshoras vs la tranquilidad de tener todo automatizado.

### Prompt para SeaDance:
```
Scene 1 (0-3s): Close-up of a smartphone vibrating on a nightstand in a dark bedroom. The screen shows WhatsApp notifications flooding in at 2:23 AM with urgent red alert styling. The room is dimly lit, only the phone screen illuminates the scene. Moody, cinematic lighting.

Scene 2 (4-8s): Clean, bright screen recording style. A WhatsApp chat interface on a teal-and-white background. A patient messages "Quiero turno para mañana". Instantly, an elegant chatbot responds with available time slots in a clean, modern interface. Fast, smooth animation. Professional medical aesthetic.

Scene 3 (9-15s): A calm, professional doctor sitting in a modern, bright clinic office. Morning sunlight through windows. The doctor is relaxed, checking a full calendar on a tablet, smiling subtly. A steaming mate cup on the desk. Clean, minimalist medical environment with soft teal accents.

Scene 4 (16-20s): Clean white background (#f7f9fb). Centered text: "Menos inasistencias, más consultas" in bold dark teal. Below, a subtle CTA button: "Agendar reunión sin costo" in teal. Soft shadow, modern sans-serif typography. Fade in smoothly.

Style: Medical professional, clean aesthetic, bright and trustworthy, teal and white color palette, 9:16 vertical format, 60fps smooth transitions.
```

## NOTAS TÉCNICAS PARA SEEDANCE

### Paleta de colores obligatoria:
- **Fondo principal:** `#f7f9fb` (blanco azulado claro)
- **Acentos:** `#00685f` (teal médico)
- **Texto:** `#191c1e` (casi negro)
- **Éxitos/confirmaciones:** `#008378` (teal claro)
- **Alertas/urgencias:** `#ba1a1a` (rojo médico, usar con moderación)

### Elementos visuales consistentes:
- WhatsApp burbujas de chat en verde estándar (#25D366) o teal (#00685f)
- Interfaz de chat limpia, moderna, sin clutter
- Calendarios con checkmarks en teal
- Doctores con bata blanca, entorno médico moderno y limpio
- Nada de morado, violeta, o colores oscuros de fondo

### Estilo general:
- **Iluminación:** Brillante, natural, confiable
- **Mood:** Profesional, calmado, eficiente, médico
- **Evitar:** Fondos oscuros, neon, colores saturados, estética "startup tech"
- **Priorizar:** Claridad, confianza, higiene visual, profesionalismo médico
